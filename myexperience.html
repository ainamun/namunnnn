My Experience

"My experiences have taught me a lot and I'm HAPPY with my learnings."



During the last semester break, I as a student of library management has an industrial training in the library for 6 weeks.

So, I chose Library of Tun Seri Lanang that was located in Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia (UKM) for my internship place.

I went there with my friends which is Amirah Zakiah and Anis Aida.


For 6 weeks working in that library, I have been learned and taught a lot of things. Through this industrial learning, it helps

me to improve my knowledge and help me to improve in subject cataloguing. Besides, it give me a good memories because the librarians

and the staff are very nice to me.


The things that I have been taught such as how to catalogue books, how to put the book on the shelves using call number. Besides, I am

also being taught how to input data into library system and many more. I think this will help me when I start working soon. This is

one of the best experienced that I ever had in my life.
